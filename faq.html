Task Management System - FAQs
What is the Task Management System?
It is a simple web app to create, track, update, and delete your tasks efficiently.
Do I need to register to use the system?
Yes, you must register and log in to access your personalized dashboard.
Can I edit or delete my tasks?
Absolutely! You can update task details or delete tasks anytime from your dashboard.
Is my task data secure?
Your data is saved securely and only accessible when you log in.
